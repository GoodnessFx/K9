- GDELT country-pair tension scores -> predict regulatory risk per jurisdiction.
- Internet outage tracking (Cloudflare Radar) -> on-chain activity disruption signals.

- Country Risk Score for regulatory exposure (which chains/protocols are in risk jurisdictions).
- Supply chain disruption signals -> commodity crypto correlations.

## Phase 4 — Prediction Markets Layer
- Polymarket integration: deep volume flow analysis.
- Metaculus research-grade predictions and Manifold Markets.
- Prediction-leads-news correlation (when market moves before news breaks = signal).

## Phase 5 — NFT & Social Intelligence

- Wallet clustering: track wallets that consistently buy early.
- Social graph analysis: which influencers' followers buy what.
- Discord/Telegram server growth tracking for protocols.

## Phase 6 — Institutional Intelligence
- 13F filing analysis: what are top funds adding/removing.
- Grayscale premium/discount and CME futures basis/open interest.
- CFTC commitment of traders reports.
- Stablecoin treasury movements (Tether, Circle reserve reports).
